REASONING TRACE FOR: CSV parser bugs

PROBLEM DECOMPOSITION:
- Two bugs: (1) last row dropped if no trailing newline, (2) escaped quotes not handled

WHAT WAS TRIED:
- Traced through the parser character by character
- The loop ends without appending the last field/row if there's no final newline

KEY INSIGHT:
- After the loop ends, there may be a pending field and row that never got appended
- For escaped quotes: two consecutive quote chars inside a quoted field should produce one quote in output, not toggle in_quotes twice

PITFALLS:
- Be careful with the quote toggling — need to look ahead or track state differently for escaped quotes
- Edge case: empty last field with no trailing newline
